Features

Everything you need to analyse your pace

All the data the game generates, presented in a clean browser dashboard — on any device on your local network.

🗺

Live track map

Animated car position plotted in real time. Toggle between sector colouring or a speed heatmap. Step through any previous lap with the arrow navigation.

📈

Live telemetry charts

Speed trace, throttle & brake inputs, and a G-force circle updated every 250 ms. Overlay two laps to compare them side-by-side.

⚖

Lap comparison

Click any two laps to overlay their speed, inputs, and G-force traces. Spot exactly where time is gained or lost corner by corner.

👑

Career stats

Tracks race results across every session — wins, podiums, points total, DNFs, grid vs finish positions, and fastest lap awards.

▶

Auto-capture

Lap times recorded the instant you cross the finish line — including the final lap. No button presses, no setup per session.

⏱

Sector splits

S1, S2, and S3 times per lap. Best sector each session highlighted in purple so weak points are immediately obvious.

🚗

Tyre compound tracking

Soft, Medium, Hard, Inter, and Wet — each lap records the compound with colour-coded pills in the table.

★

Personal bests

Session best highlighted in gold. All-time track PBs stored per track and session type — Monaco Qualifying and Monaco Race kept separately.

🏁

Team themes

Choose a colour theme for all 10 F1 2025 constructors from a dropdown in the header. Preference is saved in the browser.

🔔

Toast notifications

Instant pop-up alerts when you set a new track PB or beat your best sector time.

💾

Persistent storage

Every session and lap saved automatically to a local SQLite database. Data survives restarts. Export any session as CSV.

🚀

One-click launchers

Double-click to start on Windows, macOS, or Linux. Your browser opens automatically — no terminal needed.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

Pitwall IQ uses F1 25's built-in UDP telemetry — no mods or extra software required.

01

Enable UDP in-game

Go to Settings → Telemetry Settings. Set UDP Telemetry to On, IP to 127.0.0.1, and port to 20777. One-time setup.

02

Launch the app

Double-click the launcher for your platform — Windows (.bat), macOS (.command), or Linux (.sh). Your browser opens automatically.

03

Enter a session

Load into any session in F1 25. The dashboard status switches to LIVE TELEMETRY and data starts populating immediately.

04

Drive normally

Every lap, sector, and tyre is captured automatically. AI debrief and community leaderboard are built in — no setup needed.

Built-in extras

More than just lap times

Both features are built in and ready to use — no configuration or account required.

Built-in • No setup

AI Lap Debrief

Click AI DEBRIEF at any point during or after a session to get a race-engineer-style analysis — pace consistency, sector weaknesses, tyre performance, and one actionable recommendation. 10 debriefs per day, no API key needed.

Opt-in • No account

Community Leaderboard

Compare your track PBs against other Pitwall IQ users. The top-25 leaderboard appears live in the dashboard. Enable the SUBMIT PBs toggle to participate. Your identity is an anonymous UUID — no sign-up required.
